# Product Context

## Vision
To provide a robust, scalable platform for San Francisco and Bay Area community creators, enabling them to build, manage, and grow their projects and businesses effectively.

## Core Value Proposition
- **For Entrepreneurs:** Tools to launch and manage business checklists, track progress, and access resources.
- **For Community Leaders:** Capabilities to oversee member growth, manage partner relationships, and ensure compliance.

## Target Users
- **Primary:** Local entrepreneurs and small business owners.
- **Secondary:** Community organizers and non-profit leaders.

## Key Success Metrics
- **North Star Metric:** Growth in the member database.
- **Secondary Metrics:** Number of active projects/businesses launched, Partner Portal engagement.

## Geographic Focus
- **Initial:** The broader Bay Area (San Francisco and surrounding regions).

## Design & Experience
- **Aesthetic:** Modern, clean, and professional (Enterprise-grade).
- **Theme:** Consistent with existing branding (Flight Deck), utilizing Shadcn UI and Tailwind CSS.
- **UX Goal:** Intuitive, efficient, and reliable for professional use.

## Features Focus
- **Current Priority:** Enhancing the Partner Portal and member dashboard functionality.
- **Future:** Marketplace for services, deeper community integration.
